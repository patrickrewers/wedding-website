What Information Do We Need?

1. Attendance Status

Please let us know if you'll be able to join us or if you must regretfully decline. We understand that circumstances change, and we appreciate any response.

2. Full Names

Please provide the full names of all guests attending from your party. This helps us create accurate place cards and seating arrangements.

3. Number of Guests

Confirm the total number of people attending from your invitation. Please only include those listed on your formal invitation.

4. Mailing Address

We need your current mailing address to send thank-you notes and any future correspondence.

5. Dietary Restrictions

Please inform us of any dietary restrictions, food allergies, or special meal requirements. We want to ensure everyone can enjoy the meal! Common restrictions include vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, nut allergies, shellfish allergies, etc.

6. Song Request (Optional)

Help us create the perfect playlist! Let us know what song will get you out on the dance floor.

Important Reminders

  • RSVP Deadline: Please respond by August 1, 2026
  • Guest Count: Please only include guests specifically named on your invitation
  • Changes: If your plans change after you RSVP, please let us know as soon as possible

Unable to Attend?

We understand that not everyone will be able to join us, and we completely understand. If you're unable to attend, we'd still love to hear from you! Please send us a brief email letting us know you won't be able to make it.

Even if you can't be there in person, you'll be in our hearts on our special day.

After You RSVP

Once we receive your RSVP, we'll send you a confirmation email. As the wedding date approaches, we'll send additional details about the ceremony, reception timeline, and any other important information.

If you don't receive a confirmation within 5 business days, please reach out to ensure we received your response.
