Dress Code

We request formal attire for our wedding celebration. Think elegant cocktail dresses, suits, and dress shoes. We want everyone to feel comfortable and confident while looking their best!

Appropriate Attire ✓

Here are some examples of appropriate formal attire for our wedding:

Cocktail dress or elegant knee-length/midi dress

Floor-length formal gown

Dark suit with dress shirt and tie

Formal suit or tuxedo

Women's Attire Guidelines

Recommended:

  • Cocktail dresses or formal evening gowns
  • Dressy jumpsuits or elegant pantsuits
  • Knee-length, midi, or floor-length dresses
  • Dress shoes or elegant heels (wedges recommended for outdoor ceremony)
  • Sophisticated accessories and jewelry
  • Light shawl or jacket for evening

Color Suggestions:

Feel free to wear any colors except white, ivory, cream, or champagne (reserved for the bride). Jewel tones, pastels, and bold colors are all welcome!

Men's Attire Guidelines

Recommended:

  • Dark or navy suits with dress shirt and tie
  • Tuxedos (optional but welcome)
  • Dress pants with blazer/sport coat
  • Polished dress shoes
  • Pocket square or tie clip for extra style
  • Dress socks (no white athletic socks)

Please Avoid ✗

To maintain the formal atmosphere, please avoid the following:

Jeans, casual pants, or shorts

T-shirts, polo shirts, or sneakers

White, ivory, or cream colored dresses

Beach wear, flip-flops, or overly casual attire

Additional Guidelines

What to Avoid:

  • Denim or jeans of any kind
  • Athletic wear or sneakers
  • Overly casual shirts (t-shirts, polo shirts, Hawaiian shirts)
  • Shorts, capris, or cargo pants
  • Flip-flops, sandals, or beach footwear
  • White, ivory, cream, or champagne-colored dresses
  • Overly revealing or club-style attire
  • Work uniforms or overly casual business attire

Pro Tips

  • Outdoor Ceremony: The ceremony will be on grass, so consider wedge heels or block heels instead of stilettos
  • Weather: June evenings can cool down, so bring a light jacket, wrap, or shawl
  • Comfort: You'll be dancing! Choose shoes you can move in comfortably
  • When in Doubt: Err on the side of being more formal rather than less

Need Help?

If you're unsure about whether your outfit is appropriate, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at wedding@example.com. We're happy to help!
