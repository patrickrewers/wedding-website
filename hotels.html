Things to Do in Ventura County

While you're in town for our wedding, we encourage you to explore the beautiful area! Here are some recommendations:

  • 29th Annual Fall Harvest Festival: Walk the corn maze, carve pumpkins, and enjoy many other great seasonal activities
  • Oceanside Fun: Enjoy one of the many beautiful beaches on the Southern California coastline
  • Haunted House: Get into the holiday spirit with one of the best haunted houses in SoCal
