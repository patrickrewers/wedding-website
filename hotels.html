Alternative Accommodations

If our room blocks are full or you prefer alternative lodging, the Napa Valley area offers many wonderful options including:

  • Vacation Rentals: Airbnb and VRBO have numerous properties in the area
  • Boutique Hotels: Many charming bed & breakfasts and boutique hotels throughout wine country
  • Downtown Napa: Several hotels along the Napa River with easy access to restaurants and shops

Please note that our venue is located in a rural area, so having transportation is recommended if you stay at alternative accommodations.

Transportation Tips

Uber/Lyft: Ride-sharing services are available throughout Napa Valley, though wait times may be longer in rural areas. We recommend arranging transportation in advance.

Designated Drivers: If you're driving, please designate a non-drinking driver for your safety. We want all our guests to enjoy the celebration responsibly.

Shuttle Service: The Harvest Inn offers complimentary shuttle service to the venue upon request. Please arrange this in advance with the hotel concierge.

Things to Do in Napa Valley

While you're in town for our wedding, we encourage you to explore the beautiful Napa Valley! Here are some recommendations:

  • Wine Tasting: Visit world-renowned wineries and vineyards
  • Culinary Experiences: Enjoy farm-to-table dining at exceptional restaurants
  • Hot Air Balloon Rides: Take in stunning views of wine country from above
  • Spa Treatments: Relax with wine-inspired spa services
  • Oxbow Public Market: Browse local artisan foods and crafts in downtown Napa
  • Napa Valley Wine Train: Experience a scenic gourmet journey through the valley
